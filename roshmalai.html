Sweet Crush

Rosh Malai!!

Ingredients:



For Sweets:
Milk 1 liter
Cornstarch 1 tsp
Vinegar 4 tbsp + 1/4 cup of water

For Sugar Syrup:
Water 4 cups
Sugar 1 cup

For Malai:
Milk 3 cups
Sugar 2 tbsp

How to Make:

For Sweets:

1. Pour 1 liter of milk in a heavy bottom pan and bring it to a boil.
2. When it starts boiling, turn off the heat and wait for 2-3 minutes.
3. Mix the vinegar with water and pour 2 tablespoons at a time in the milk and stir gently.
4. When the milk has curdled completely, pour it into a cheesecloth, and hang it for 40-50 min.
5. Take the chana on a plate and add 1 tsp cornstarch. Knead for 4-5 min and make small oval-shaped sweets.
Sugar Syrup:

1. Add 4 cups of water and add 1 cup of sugar in a pan and bring it to a boil. 2. Now add the sweets to the syrup and cook for 20 min over med heat. 3. Once cooked, place them in a bowl and cool them completely. For Malai:

1. Add 3 cups of milk in a heavy bottom pan and heat until it reduces to half (stir constantly). 2. Add 2 tbsp of sugar, cardamom, almonds, and pistachio. 3. Squeeze out the syrup from the sweets and bring them to the Malai. 4. Enjoy
